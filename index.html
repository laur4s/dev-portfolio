Thats me!

Hi, I am Laura. Currently I am pursuing a master's degree in 'Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence' at TUM and am really enthusiastic about robots. My primary interest lies in Human-Robot Interaction, with a focus on how collaborative robots can be integrated into everyday environments in a meaningful and effective way. Driven by demographic change and increasing labor shortages, I see HRI as a key research area for enabling robots to work safely and intuitively alongside humans. My work explores practical implementations of these ideas through programming projects with collaborative robots, including interaction, manipulation, and impedance-based control.
